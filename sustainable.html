We strive to implement environmental improvements that lead to a sustainable future and result in social and economic improvements in the world around us. This mission is most important to us, it is a continuous journey that never ends. We strive to improve ourselves every day and therefore rely on these three sustainability pillars: Clean, Conscious & Caring.

MAIN GOALS

We stay on the right path to sustainability with the following main goals:

- Before the year 2023, all formulas contain more than 90% ingredients of natural origin.
- In the same year we hope to have reached 2 million children with our yoga & mindfulness program Super Chill.
- Another milestone is 2025, when we only have products in our range that are refillable, recyclable and / or made from recycled materials.
